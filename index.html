Objectives

Objectives

This project aims to create a compelling and user-centric digital platform that effectively conveyed the organization's mission, while engaging and empowering users to take action in support of animal welfare. The key objectives included enhancing the overall user experience, increasing conversion rates, and establishing a strong and memorable brand identity that instilled trust and compassion in the hearts of visitors.

This project aims to create a compelling and user-centric digital platform that effectively conveyed the organization's mission, while engaging and empowering users to take action in support of animal welfare. The key objectives included enhancing the overall user experience, increasing conversion rates, and establishing a strong and memorable brand identity that instilled trust and compassion in the hearts of visitors.

Research: The UX designer embarked on extensive research to understand the non-profit sector and the specific challenges faced by the ASPCA organization. They conducted interviews with key stakeholders, gathered user feedback, and analyzed existing user data to identify pain points and user preferences within the animal welfare domain. This research phase provided valuable insights into the needs of both the organization and its target audience.

  1. Branding:

    Building upon the research findings, the designer developed a refreshed brand identity for ASPCA that aligned with their mission and resonated with their audience. They created a new logo that conveyed compassion and trust, selecting a color scheme and typography that evoked a sense of warmth and empathy. By crafting a visual style guide, complete with imagery and graphics that showcased the connection between animals and humans, the designer ensured a consistent and compelling brand experience throughout the website.


  2. Content:

    Understanding the importance of engaging and informative content, the designer created compelling narratives, powerful visuals, and impactful calls-to-action. They worked closely with the ASPCA team to develop comprehensive destination guides, adoption resources, and educational materials that catered to diverse user segments. By tailoring the content to various types of visitors, such as potential adopters, donors, and volunteers, the designer aimed to provide a seamless and meaningful experience for each user.


  3. Information Architecture:

    With a focus on usability and clarity, the designer crafted an information architecture that made it easy for users to navigate the ASPCA website. They categorized content into logical sections, including adoption, volunteer opportunities, and educational resources, ensuring intuitive navigation and accessibility of crucial information. The designer also implemented user-friendly search functionalities and clear pathways to streamline user journeys.


  4. Wireframes:

    To visualize the new website structure, the designer created wireframes that showcased the layout, organization, and functionality of the ASPCA website. These wireframes served as a blueprint for the development process, allowing for early testing of navigation and content placement. The wireframes also integrated visual design elements, such as images and graphics, providing a glimpse into the overall aesthetic and user experience.


  5. Prototyping:

    Leveraging design tools like Figma, the designer created high-fidelity prototypes that brought the ASPCA website to life. These prototypes were tested with users to gather feedback on the design, usability, and emotional resonance. By incorporating visual design elements like captivating imagery, compelling animations, and seamless interactions, the designer aimed to create an immersive and emotionally engaging experience for users.


  6. Development:

    Once the design and prototype were finalized, the designer collaborated closely with a developer to bring the redesigned ASPCA website to fruition. The development phase focused on optimizing speed, performance, and accessibility. Features such as a search bar, user reviews, and social sharing buttons were carefully integrated to enhance user engagement and interaction. The designer ensured that the visual design was implemented accurately and effectively, resulting in a polished and impactful digital presence for ASPCA.

Featured Stories

This section on the ASPCA website redesign project provides users with inspiring and heartwarming narratives that showcase the impact of their support and highlight successful animal rescue stories. By sharing these stories, users are not only emotionally engaged but also motivated to contribute, volunteer, or adopt, knowing that their involvement can make a tangible difference in the lives of animals in need.

Our Voice

This section serves as a powerful platform for users to connect with the organization on a deeper level. By sharing their motivations and mission statement, as well as showcasing the impactful work they have accomplished, users gain a comprehensive understanding of the ASPCA's dedication to animal welfare and are inspired to join the cause, knowing they are supporting an organization with a proven track record of making a difference in the lives of countless animals.

Donations Page

This Project offers users a streamlined and secure platform to contribute towards the welfare of animals. With clear and transparent information about how their donations will be used, users can feel confident that their financial support directly impacts the rescue, care, and advocacy efforts carried out by the ASPCA, enabling them to make a meaningful difference in the lives of animals in need.

The ASPCA website and brand redesign project successfully revitalized the digital presence of this vital non-profit organization. Through meticulous research, thoughtful design, and collaboration with stakeholders, the UX designer was able to create a website that not only captured the essence of ASPCA's mission but also provided a seamless and engaging user experience. The new brand identity instilled a sense of trust and compassion, while the intuitive information architecture and user-friendly features empowered users to explore adoption options, access valuable resources, and contribute to the cause. Ultimately, the project aimed to make a positive impact by connecting animals in need with caring individuals, and the redesigned ASPCA website now serves as a powerful tool to drive awareness, support, and positive change in the world of animal welfare.
